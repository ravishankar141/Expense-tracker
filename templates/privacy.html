Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account, including your name, email address, and password. We also collect data about your expenses, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. This information is necessary to provide you with our expense tracking service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service. We use your account information to authenticate you and maintain your session. Your expense data is stored to display your spending history, generate reports, and provide insights into your financial patterns. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our database with appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, or other operational purposes. These services have access to your data only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose. We currently do not share your financial data with any external marketing services.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us. We are committed to addressing your privacy concerns and will respond to inquiries in a timely manner.
